Bayesian Regression

How can we create a model when we have random data, and we are unsure about the links between each point? Moreovoer, how can we use intuition to guide our decision making process? Bayesian regression uses the ideal of "intuition" or belief, into model creation. We can do this by applying this belief in the form of "weights" on coefficients of the final model.
